# Image Generation Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photo of a tromba marina concept, a tall narrow bowed monochord-family instrument with a long single main string, a resonant wooden body, simple neck or string path marked subtly for natural harmonic reference, and a distinctive asymmetrical buzzing trumpet bridge with one normal foot and one lightly coupled loose foot against the soundboard. Optional fine sympathetic strings are visible inside or along the body as a restrained resonance branch. Warm wood, simple historical-workshop finish, no claims of measured reconstruction. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop test scene of a tromba marina buzzing-bridge bench rig beside the tall instrument concept: a single tensioned string over an interchangeable trumpet bridge on a small soundboard coupon, with shims and contact-material samples laid out. Natural light, bow nearby, a musician testing harmonic touch positions without pressing the string to a fingerboard, careful prototype mood.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the trumpet bridge mechanism: one bridge foot firmly carrying string load, the other loose foot barely touching the soundboard through a tiny adjustable shim, the single bowed string crossing the bridge crown, and sympathetic strings softly blurred inside the body. Shallow depth of field, high detail, realistic wood grain and contact wear.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, fretted guitar necks, multi-string cello layouts, fake historical labels, impossible buzzing bridge geometry, extra limbs, distorted hands, and uncontrolled broken-looking parts.
